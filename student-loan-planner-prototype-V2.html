Prototype limitations and disclosure: This prototype uses a simplified browser-side simulation to help test interaction design and comparison structure. It is not a substitute for official Federal Student Aid guidance, Department of Education regulations, or your loan servicer’s determination. Eligibility for PAYE, ICR, RAP, IBR, and Parent PLUS transitions can depend on loan-level and historical facts not fully modeled here. Use this prototype for layout, flow, and explanation testing, then replace the engine with a regulation-tested rules module before public release.

Built as a self-contained single-file HTML prototype. No external libraries required.
