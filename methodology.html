Methodology

The estimator validates borrower inputs, evaluates every active rule-set plan, simulates monthly repayment, ranks clearly eligible plans for a top-three comparison, and preserves potentially eligible or informational plans in the grouped all-plan view.

Monthly simulation

Each monthly record tracks beginning balance, interest accrual, borrower payment, interest paid, principal paid, unpaid interest, waived interest, plan credits, ending balance, and qualifying-payment counts.

Plan formulas

Fixed, graduated, tiered standard, income-driven, ICR, RAP, income-sensitive, and Perkins formula modules are separated from DOM rendering and tied to plan source IDs.

Limitations

Results are planning estimates and are not official eligibility, tax, legal, or financial advice. Rule set: 2026-07-01. Rules reviewed: 2026-06-18.
