# Calculation Methodology

The engine normalizes borrower inputs, evaluates every registered plan, simulates eligible and potentially eligible plans month-by-month, ranks clearly eligible plans by current monthly payment and total borrower payments, and returns recommendations, warnings, sources, and assumptions.

Monthly records include beginning balance, accrued interest, borrower payment, interest paid, principal paid, unpaid interest, waived interest, plan credits, ending balance, and qualifying-payment counts where relevant.
